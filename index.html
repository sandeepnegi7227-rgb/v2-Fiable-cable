Who We Are

Built in Narela.
Trusted Across India.

"Copper is the backbone of every electrical system. We make sure every metre of it is worthy of that responsibility."
Fiable Cables Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2015 certified copper wire and cable manufacturer based at Narela Industrial Area, Delhi. Established in 1998, we have grown into one of Delhi's most respected fully integrated copper manufacturers — converting Grade A copper cathode into finished wire and cable products entirely in-house.

Our 5-acre facility houses 48 wire drawing lines, continuous casting equipment, stranding lines, and insulation extrusion lines. We supply to cable manufacturers, motor winders, EPC contractors, and exporters across India and 40+ countries.
