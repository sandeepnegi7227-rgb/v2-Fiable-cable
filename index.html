Our Story

Built in Narela.
Known across India.

What began in 1998 as a wire drawing unit in Narela Industrial Area has grown into one of Delhi's most respected copper wire manufacturers — a fully integrated facility producing everything from bare conductor to finished insulated cable under one roof.

"Copper is a living material. Every micron of its processing defines whether it carries power or fails under load."
Our plant spans over 5 acres, houses 48 drawing lines, and operates continuous casting equipment that converts copper cathode into rod stock in a single controlled pass. We supply bare wire, stranded conductors, winding wire, power cable, and flexible/control cables to cable manufacturers, motor winders, switchgear makers, EPC contractors, and exporters across India and 40+ countries.

Manufacturing Process

From cathode to finished reel.

Cathode Receipt
Grade A copper cathode (≥99.97% Cu) received, sampled, and batch-recorded against supplier certificate.
Casting & Rolling
Continuously cast and hot-rolled into 8mm copper rod under controlled conditions.
Wire Drawing
48 lines reduce rod 8mm → 0.05mm with inline annealing to restore ductility.
Stranding & Insulation
Wires stranded to spec, insulated with PVC, XLPE, LSZH, or enamel per product type.
QC & Dispatch
100% electrical & spark testing, certificates issued. Full batch traceability. 48hr despatch.

Why Fiable Cables
